Instructions

Use A and D to move the ship left and right.

Shields grant invulnerability to damage.

Aliens give points:

  • Bronze: 20 points
  • Silver: 50 points
  • Gold: 100 points

Colliding with asteroids deducts 10 points.

Robots deduct 50 points.

Electric fences prevent you from shooting.

Clicking on a UFO gives 50 points.

After each lap, speed increases by 10%.

Press C to switch the camera from chase view to overview and vice versa.
